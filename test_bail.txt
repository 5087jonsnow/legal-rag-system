SUPREME COURT OF INDIA
AIR 2024 SC 1001

Bail Application
Section 438 CrPC

The petitioner seeks anticipatory bail in connection with FIR No. 123/2024.

HELD: After considering the facts and circumstances, anticipatory bail is granted subject to the following conditions:

1. The petitioner shall cooperate with the investigation
2. The petitioner shall not tamper with evidence
3. The petitioner shall appear before the investigating officer when required

Order accordingly.
